DESCRIPTION(S.B.S.S.U)

Shaheed Bhagat Singh State University , formerly known as Shaheed Bhagat Singh State Technical Campus and Shaheed Bhagat Singh College of Engineering and Technology, is a state university in Ferozepur, Punjab.In 2021 it got University status and name of Shaheed Bhagat Singh State University by Punjab act no. 10 of 2021.The university is AICTE approved, Academically Autonomous under UGC act and was affiliated to I. K. Gujral Punjab Technical University, Jalandhar and follows semester system of education. Each academic year is divided into two semester- July/August to December and January to June/July. University examinations are held at the end of each semester.stablished in 1995, Shaheed Bhagat Singh State Technical Campus is a premier Institute located in Punjab. Institute offers Degree and Diploma courses including 11 UG and 6 PG courses. Additionally, it also has After 10th Diploma courses. These programs are offerred in Full Time mode. Shaheed Bhagat Singh State Technical Campus is a premier institute for B.Sc., BCA, B.E. / B.Tech, MBA/PGDM, B.Arch, MCA, M.E./M.Tech, After 1th Diploma courses. Candidate can pursue in multiple range of courses like B.Sc., BCA, B.E. / B.Tech, MBA/PGDM, B.Arch, MCA, M.E./M.Tech, After 1th Diploma with the stream of IT and Software, Engineering, Business and Management Studies, Architecture and Planning. Over the period, Shaheed Bhagat Singh State Technical Campus has gained expertize through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of Chemical Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering. Institue offered courses that are approved by AICTE. College offered courses with intake of 1081 candidates . Shaheed Bhagat Singh State Technical Campus offers good infrastructure like Auditorium, Boys Hostel, Cafeteria, Girls Hostel, Gym, Hospital / Medical Facilities, Hostel, Labs, Library, Sports Complex to students.
